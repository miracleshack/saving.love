How To Get To Heaven

According to the Bible:

All humans do evil.
We're sinners.
Everyone's undeserving.

  • "Nobody on earth's always good and never sins." (Ecclesiastes 7:20)
  • "For everyone's sinned and falls short of God's glory." (Romans 3:23)
  • "Everyone's turned away; all have become useless. Nobody does good, not even one.‚Äù (Psalm 53:3)

Heaven's a free gift.
Donations can't earn it.
Doing good won't save you.

  • "God saved you by grace when you believed. You can't take credit for this; it's the gift of God." (Ephesians 2:9)
  • "Peter replied: 'May your money be destroyed with you for thinking God's gift can be bought with money.' " (Acts 8:20)
  • "But we believe through the undeserved grace of the Lord Jesus Christ we'll be saved, just as they are." (Acts 15:11)

The gospel's the good news.
The gospel: Jesus died (paying sin penalty), got buried, and was risen.
He died in our place.

  • "He was handed over to die for our sins, and was raised to make us right with God." (Romans 4:25)
  • "What's most important I received and delivered to you: Christ died for our sins, as the Scriptures said; that he was buried, and that he rose again the third day according to the Scriptures:" (1 Corinthians 15:3-4)
  • "At just the right time, when we were still helpless, Christ died for the ungodly." (Romans 5:6)

Jesus died, paying our sin penalty.

Heaven doesn't
 cost money.

Only trust the good news alone for Heaven.
False gospels are trusting other things
for Heaven, which doesn't save.

  • "Jesus answered: 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me.' " (John 14:6)
  • "Salvation's in nobody else: God hasn't given another name under Heaven by which we must be saved." (Acts 4:12)
  • "That's why I said you'll die in your sins: unless you believe that I Am, you'll die in your sins." (John 8:24)

Heaven isn't accepted by trusting Jesus died for sin, got buried, and was risen plus trusting good deeds... that's a false gospel.

Doing/saying these won't earn Heaven:

  • Praying.
  • Water baptism.
  • Donations/tithing.
  • Feeling sorry for sin.
  • Giving your life to God.
  • Confessing "Jesus is Lord."
  • Inviting Jesus into your heart/life.
  • Planning to follow Jesus at any cost.
  • Turning from sin or trying not to sin.
  • Confessing sin, "mortal sins," or "deadly sins."
  • Stopping certain sins, "mortal sins," or "deadly sins."
  • Sharing your trust/faith/belief in Jesus without denying it.

Accepting the good news of Jesus Christ is trust/faith/belief in only the gospel alone.
